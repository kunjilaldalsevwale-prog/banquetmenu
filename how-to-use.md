# 🏛 Food House — Menu Manager
### Team Food House | Digital QR Menu System

---

## ✅ HOW TO USE (No technical knowledge needed)

### STEP 1 — Open the app
Double-click **index.html** to open it in your browser (use Chrome for best results)

### STEP 2 — Upload your menus
- Click **"+ Upload PDF / Image"** under Restaurant, Banquet Hall, or Chaat Adda
- Select your approved menu PDF or image file
- It uploads instantly and shows in Customer View

### STEP 3 — Download your QR codes
- Scroll down to the **QR Codes** section
- Click **"⬇ Download QR PNG"** for each menu
- Print the QR and place on your tables

### STEP 4 — Customer scans QR
- Customer scans QR → sees your latest menu on their phone
- Every time you upload a new menu, it updates automatically

## 📱 TO SHARE WITH YOUR TEAM

**Option A — WhatsApp the file**
Send the index.html file to your team on WhatsApp.
They open it on their phone/laptop and can upload menus.

**Option B — Host it online (Recommended for full team access)**
1. Go to netlify.com/drop
2. Drag and drop the entire "fh-app" folder
3. Get a live link like: foodhouse.netlify.app
4. Share that link with your team
5. QR codes will point to that live link
6. Your team opens the link on ANY phone, anywhere
